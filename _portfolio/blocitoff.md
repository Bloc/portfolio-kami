## Explanation

This is my first Bloc project, a self destructing list, using Ruby on Rails.

## Problem

I needed to provide a way of keeping a list of tasks with a deadline. We don't want the list to grow forever, so as tasks are completed, they are removed from the list.  To keep information clean and secure, we'll need to create profiles for users, within their account they can create as many lists as they want.
